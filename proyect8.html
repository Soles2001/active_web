VALENCIA WINES
(ART DIRECTION / BRANDING)

Each wine in this collection is inspired by Valencian festivities, reflecting the unique character of each one. The designs feature a tile style, inspired by the typical hydraulic tiles used in the traditional houses of Valencia. The Fallera wine presents a smooth and elegant taste, evoking the fine dresses of the Falleras and the air of sophistication of this celebration.

On the other hand, La Tomatina wine and San Juan wine stand out for their intense and vibrant flavors, capturing the energy and passion of these festivities, such as the momentum of jumping bonfires or immersing oneself in the tomato battle.
