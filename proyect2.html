ADN FORUM

ADN Forum is the first exhibition of Design and Instalative Art in Maritime Containers held in Madrid, offering designers and artists the opportunity to show their work in an unconventional environment. The maritime container intervened by the students of UDIT will be covered by a mural designed by Daniel Alcolea and myself, students of UDIT. Alcolea and myself, students of the Multimedia and Graphic Design Degree of the university, together with Blanca Oriz and Aníbal de Andrade, graduates in Multimedia and Graphic Design at UDIT.

“The world is full of things you don't see.” Thanks to design, it is possible to filter out the noise of the reality around us. around us. In this case, capsules have been created that give the power to decode what cannot be seen with the naked eye. the naked eye. By immersing themselves in each of these capsules, visitors can discover the design process in its entirety. in its entirety.
