EDICIONES COMISURA
(WEB DESIGN / WEB DEVELOPMENT)

A project developed in Mondo Estudio, in which we were in charge of redesigning the e-commerce of this independent publishing house. A previous design and prototyping was done in Figma, and then dumped into WrodPress using Astra, Spectra and Woocomerce plugins.

6 pages were developed, along with the cart and product pages. A section for subscribing to the editorial itself was also created.
